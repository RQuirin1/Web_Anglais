Biography

Léo Valdez is born in 1976, in Bratislava, Slovakia. He spent most of his life here, with his beloved mother and sister, Maria and Lucia Valdez. He left in 1995 in order to study in France, where he lived for 15 years. He started writing in this period, starting by short novels, published on blogs mostly. His true work started in 2011, when he comes back to Slovakia, in order to watch after his mother, weakened by illness. We write about 5 poetry collections, and his most known series of novels, " The Haunted Hills".
